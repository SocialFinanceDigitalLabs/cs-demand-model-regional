Adjust weekly costs


You may want to edit the weekly cost of a child placement to give a more accurate picture of your projected spend.

  • New costs will overwrite previous values.
  • Proportions of placement types for each proportion must sum to 1.
  • Entering proportions that sum to more than 1 for each placement will lead to adjustments.
  • Weight will be given to user entered adjustments. e.g. if you change a single placement type to 0.9, the remaining proportions will be adjusted down to allow for this.
